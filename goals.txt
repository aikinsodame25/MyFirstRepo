My Personal and Academic Goals for Next Year
Personal Goals
• Build stronger study habits. Set fixed hours for reading each day.
• Improve physical fitness. Exercise three times per week.
• Strengthen communication skills. Practice clear speaking and writing through school projects.
• Grow your network. Join one new club or community group.
Academic Goals
• Raise your grades in core subjects. Track your weekly progress.
• Improve your digital skills. Complete one online course in IT or cybersecurity.
• Develop research skills. Work on one long project that uses sources, data, and analysis.
• Prepare for university applications. Collect transcripts, write your statements, and meet deadlines.
